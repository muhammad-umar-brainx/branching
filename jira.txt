*Jira Shortcuts*
To Work with individual issue:
   C Create ticket
   A Assign issue to someone
   I Issue assigned to me
   B Open Issue comment
Navigate across JIRA
   G then I Find issues
   G then P Browse to project
   G then A go to Jira board
   / Search projects
   \ Stared and recent
Boast Agility
   1 go to backlog
   2 go to active log
   3 go to report
